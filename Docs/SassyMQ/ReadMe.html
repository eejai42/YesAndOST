There are 18 messages in this lexicon sent by 2 different actors who speak and 2 actors who can hear messages.

Person

Person can say

  1. "Hello" to ARMediator in the general channel.
  2. "See People" to ARMediator in the general channel.
  3. "Start Conversation" to ARMediator in the conversation channel.
  4. "Yes And" to ARMediator in the chat channel.
  5. "Yes But" to ARMediator in the chat channel.
  6. "No And" to ARMediator in the chat channel.
  7. "No But" to ARMediator in the chat channel.
  8. "Suggest Topic" to ARMediator in the conversation channel.
  9. "Suggest More Specifically" to ARMediator in the conversation channel.
  10. "Request Agreement" to ARMediator in the agreement channel.
  11. "Suggest Abstraction" to ARMediator in the abstraction channel.
  12. "Suggest Change In Abstraction" to ARMediator in the abstraction channel.
  13. "Request Focus" to ARMediator in the agreement channel.
  14. "Suggest Abstraction Removal" to ARMediator in the general channel.
  15. "Direct Message" to ARMediator in the chat channel.

Person can hear

  1. "Conversation Requested" from ARMediator.
  2. "Agreement Requested" from ARMediator.
  3. "D M For You" from ARMediator.

ARMediator

ARMediator can say

  1. "Conversation Requested" to Person in the conversation channel.
  2. "Agreement Requested" to Person in the agreement channel.
  3. "D M For You" to Person in the chat channel.
